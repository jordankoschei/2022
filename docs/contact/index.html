Contact

Want to get in touch? Great — I love hearing from people. This form goes straight to my inbox. No spammers, please!

I'd love to hear from you if:

  • You’re interesting in having me consult for your company or organization.
  • You’re just starting out and are looking for career advice.
  • You live in (or are passing through) the Hudson Valley and would like to talk shop.
  • You’d like me to write for your publication. (Paid writing only, please.)
  • You want to discuss something I wrote, share something relevant, or have a civil discussion about why you disagree.
  • You’re interested in having me speak at your event.

I probably won't respond if:

  • You're looking for a quick/cheap website.
  • You want to argue over something I wrote.
  • You're trying to sell me your SEO or marketing services.
